Jeroen Famaey

I am a research professor at the University of Antwerp and IMEC, and a member of the IDLab research group. I model, design, and optimize future wireless networks and connected applications. I am particularly interested in zero-energy computing and communications to enable a more sustainable Internet of Things, as well as challenging connected applications enabled by future wireless networks, such as networked extended reality, collaborative robot swarms, and distributed edge AI.

Selected Projects

Networked VR

Sustainable IoT

Creating a sustainble Internet of Things by replacing batteries with renewable energy sources.

Nanoscale Systems

Designing computing and networking solutions for swarms of tiny devices the size of a human cell.
