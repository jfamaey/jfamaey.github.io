ABOUT ME AWARDS TEACHING SERVICE TEAM PUBLICATIONS PROJECTS

Current Projects

AMBIENT-6G2025-2027
Towards standardized 6G connectivity for ambiently-powered energy neutral IoT devices
The AMBIENT-6G project aims to address the IoT's e-waste problem, by proposing a new class of energy-neutral IoT devices, which will significantly reduce battery waste and increase device lifetime. Such devices exploit ambient energy harvesting or intentional radio frequency wireless power transfer to achieve decades-long energy autonomy.
6G-TWIN2024-2026
Integrating Network Digital Twinning into Future AI-based 6G Systems
The overarching objective of 6G-TWIN is to provide the foundation for the design, implementation and validation of an AI-native reference architecture for 6G systems that incorporates Network Digital Twins (NDT) as a core mechanism for the end-to-end, real-time optimisation, management and control of highly dynamic and complex network scenarios.
iSee-6G2024-2026
Integrated Sensing, Energy and Communication for 6G Networks
iSEE-6G proposes a Joint Communication, Computation, Sensing, and Power Transfer (JCCSP) unified radio platform, which includes all support elements of the proposed solutions in future 6G networks. The iSEE-6G Proof-of-Concept (PoC) focuses in JCCSP use cases in aerial corridors, where UAVs with various roles providing different services coexist and coordinate with each other.
Hexa-X-II2023-2025
European level 6G Flagship project
The Smart Network and Services Joint Undertaking (SNS JU) 6G Flagship project Hexa-X-II leads the way to the end-to-end (E2E) system design (based on integrated and interacting technology enablers) and the enabling platform delivering novel services for the next generation (6G) of wireless networks.
OpenSwarm2023-2025
Orchestration and Programming of ENergy-aware and collaborative Swarms With AI-powered Reliable Methods
OpenSwarm will investigate novel methods for energy-aware, collaborative swarms of robots, UAVs and IoT devices, and validate these in real-world use cases in various application domains including cities & communities, environmental, industrial & health, and mobility.
WaveVR2022-2025
Context-Aware Millimeter Wave Network for Interactive Virtual Reality
Design of mmWave protocols optimized for VR, based on joint communications and sensing at mmWave frequencies.
LOCUSTS2022-2025
Low Latency Communication for Energy Harvesting Robot Swarms
A radically new symbol-synchronous communication approach for microsecond latency broadcast in large robot swarms.
